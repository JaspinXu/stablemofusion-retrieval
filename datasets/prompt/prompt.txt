-Goal-
Given an action description, identify the key action descriptions within it and complete the corresponding body.

-Steps-
1. Identify the key action description part of the action description, which may be a verb or a verb phrase.
- motion_desc: The description of the input text.

2. After extracting the core action description part, the information of the context should be complemented.
- context: The subject who performs the above extracted action is extracted, which may be a person, or it may be the sender or receiver of the action.

3. When finished, output {completion_delimiter}

4. You should output in this format:
("caption"{tuple_delimiter}"motion_desc"{tuple_delimiter}"Description of the action after contextual completion"){record_delimiter}

######################
-Examples-
######################
Example 1:

Text:
A person is sitting on the ground, scratches their head and looks around.
################
Output:
("caption"{tuple_delimiter}"sitting on"{tuple_delimiter}"A person is sitting on the ground."){record_delimiter}
("caption"{tuple_delimiter}"scratches their head"{tuple_delimiter}"A person scratches their head"){record_delimiter}
("caption"{tuple_delimiter}"looks around"{tuple_delimiter}"person"{tuple_delimiter}"A person looks around"){record_delimiter}
